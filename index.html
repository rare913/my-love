My Love Letter to You 💌

Even though we've never met in person, and our conversations exist only in voice messages, I feel so close to you. Hearing your voice, even through a recording, makes my heart skip and paints a smile across my day. It's amazing how words spoken softly, miles apart, can create a connection so real, so warm, that I already imagine us laughing, talking, and sharing moments together. Every message from you feels like a small bridge across the distance, carrying pieces of your heart to mine. I look forward to the day we finally meet, to see your face, to hold your hand, and to hear your voice without any screen in between. Until then, your voice is my comfort, my joy, and my reminder that love doesn't always need proximity—it just needs two hearts willing to listen, care, and believe.

Even in silence, I feel you near me; even in dreams, I find you there. The distance may be long, but my heart knows no miles—it only knows you. Every beat whispers your name, and every thought carries your smile. Love has found us in a place where eyes have not met, hands have not touched, yet the soul recognizes its counterpart. Until the day we finally share a space, your voice will be my favorite melody, your words my guiding light, and the hope of us together my strongest heartbeat. 💖
